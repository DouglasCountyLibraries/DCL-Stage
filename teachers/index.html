Teachers

We offer on-site and online services and resources especially for teachers.

Scholastic

Thousands of ready-to-go lessons, mini-books, activities and more for elementary grades.
Get Started

Scholastic

Help children build real-world knowledge using these video storybooks paired with thematically-related nonfiction ebooks.
Get Started

Page to Stage

Page to Stage brings literature to life! Every year, we bring a classic tale to schools across the county in the form of a traveling theater production especially for kids in grades K-5.

Epic Reads

Each school year, DCL library staff curate a new list of titles to recommend just-right books to your students in a fun and interactive way.

Book an Archivist

Got history? We do! Archivists and local historians from the DCL Archives & Local History can visit your school to talk about local history and how they keep track of it.

Homework HelpNow is a live homework help and tutoring service that includes tools for study and collaboration.

Students can even sharpen their cognitive skills with live chess tutoring!

Resources by Request

Resources by Request is like having a personal shopper at the library. We'll compile a set of materials on any topic, author or genre for use in your classroom—all you have to do is come pick them up.
Get Started

Library Tours

Library Tours are available to your class. Tours typically last 30 minutes, but may vary depending on the branch and services requested. Set a date to come see how things work!
Get Started

Did you know we offer other services for educators?

Stream instructional films and lessons from Kanopy and discover more great resources in our research databases.
