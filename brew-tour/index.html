BReW ToUR

Our 2022 Brew Tour includes some great Douglas County breweries and coffee shops.

Log your visits to the different participating partner establishments to win unique rewards and get entered into our Grand Prize drawing.
RegisterSign In
*Participants must register and log visits on Beanstack to be eligible for all rewards. Grand Prize drawing will be held by October 5, 2022.

Prizes & Rewards

When you log your visits to each participating partner brewery and coffee shop, you are automatically entered into our Grand Prize drawing of a Year of Beer—one premium stainless steel growler filled twice by each participating brewery.

You may also earn unique rewards by attending DCL-hosted Brew Tour events at our libraries and select brewery locations.*
*Brew Tour rewards available for pickup at your nearest DCL location.
