Reserve a Space

Douglas County Libraries offers meeting and study spaces, and indoor and outdoor event spaces, for nonprofit, community and for-profit groups.

Beginning June 3, 2024, you will need a library card and PIN to reserve rooms.
We can also help you celebrate your wedding or special occasion and baby shower!

Meeting Rooms & Event Spaces

Our vibrant buildings offer beautiful and ample meeting and event spaces, and maintain the highest standards of service. From modern furniture to high-end technology, discover the ideal event space for your next corporate function or intimate social event. We'll even host your child's birthday party!
Reserve Now

Study Rooms

Study rooms for up to four people (rooms vary by location) may be reserved up to two weeks in advance, for a maximum of two hours per day. You may forfeit your reservation if you are 15 or more minutes late. For help reserving a study room, call (303) 791-7323 or visit your library.
Reserve Now

Parker Recording Studio

Our Parker location offers state-of-the-art software and equipment customers can use to create videos, musical compositions, podcasts, marketing materials and more. Find out more about the recording studio or register now for a one hour orientation. All ages and skill levels are welcome.
Reserve Now

Upgrade Your Reservation

Our meeting rooms and event spaces accommodate the style, size and complexity of any event. When you upgrade your reservation to Select or Concierge Service, our dedicated event experts can help make your special function a success.

Self-Service Meetings & Events

  • You reserve your space online with your library card and PIN
  • Basic free meeting service during library operating hours
  • You set up and return the room to its pre-event state
  • You run your event, including managing agendas, vendors and finances
  • Drop-off/unstaffed catering permitted
Upgrade to Select or Concierge Service to sell tickets, charge an entry fee, sell goods or services, or accept donations.
Upgrade to Concierge Service to reserve 50-100% of the library, serve alcohol, or use a full-service caterer with banquet staff.

Select Service

  • You reserve your space online with your library card and PIN, at least 48 hours in advance, or contact us for details and reservations
  • Spaces may be used outside of library operating hours
  • Expert consultation from a DCL event coordinator, including final walk-through and AV troubleshooting
  • We set the room up and take it down upon your event's conclusion
  • Drop-off catering allowed; we provide recommendations if you wish
  • Supplemental furniture rental allowed through a DCL recommended vendor
  • Ticket sales/entry fees allowed
  • Sale of goods/services or receipt of donations allowed
  • Private parties/receptions allowed (without alcohol)

Concierge Service

  • We make your reservation for you, at least six weeks and up to three years in advance, and before rooms are available to the public. Contact us for details and reservations
  • Spaces may be used outside of library operating hours; 50-100% of the library may be rented
  • Full-service event planning and management, including agendas, vendors, finances and AV troubleshooting
  • We set the room up and take it down upon your event's conclusion
  • Drop-off and full-service/staffed catering (through a DCL recommended vendor) allowed
  • Supplemental furniture rental allowed through a DCL recommended vendor
  • Ticket sales/entry fees allowed
  • Sale of goods/services or receipt of donations allowed
  • Private parties/receptions allowed; alcohol may be served (some restrictions apply)
By reserving a room you agree to DCL's terms and conditions. Have questions? Call Event + Hospitality Services at (303) 688-7605.

Parker Recording Studio

    How it works:
  • Reserve the Studio for up to nine hours a week with your library card—or use one of two computer stations outside of the Studio for editing without having to reserve the Studio.
  • Store and share content to the cloud or on your flash drive or SD card.
  • Library card or photo ID required at check in.
  • Parents of children age 12 and under must reserve the Studio and remain in the room during the reservation.
  • Due to high demand, if you are more than 30 minutes late for your reservation, your time may be given to another user.
  • No food allowed. Beverages must be in a covered container.
    Imagine the possibilities:
  • Create, record and edit music, videos, family histories and more.
  • Conduct podcasts and interviews.
  • Create innovative instructional or marketing tools.
  • Craft dynamic, out-of-the-box school projects.
    Popular equipment:
  • Digital photo and video cameras, and iPads.
  • Yeti and Shure microphones and soundproofing.
  • Tripods and microphone stands.
  • Scarlet interface and Behringer mixer.
  • Electric keyboard and drum pad.
  • Green screen and lighting.
  • 27” iMacs with Retina 5K displays
    Popular software:
  • Video: iMovie, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Premiere Pro.
  • Music & Audio: GarageBand, Logic Pro, Audacity.
  • Photo & Digital Illustration: Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator.
