Reserve a Space

Douglas County Libraries offers meeting and study spaces, indoor and outdoor event spaces, and a public bandstand for performances or demonstrations, for nonprofit, community and for-profit groups.

Event Spaces & Services

Our vibrant buildings offer beautiful and ample event spaces, and maintain the highest standards of service. From modern furniture to high-end technology, discover the ideal event space for your next corporate function or intimate social event. We'll even host your child's birthday party!
Get Started

Study Rooms

Study rooms for up to eight people (rooms vary by location) may be reserved up to one month in advance, for a maximum of two hours per day. You may forfeit your reservation if you are 15 or more minutes late. For help reserving a study room, call (303) 791-7323 or visit your library.

Parker Recording Studio

Patrons at our Parker location can use state-of-the-art software and equipment to create videos, musical compositions, animations, e-books and more.

Parker Recording Studio

The Recording Studio can be reserved for up to three hours at a time. Room use can be extended if no one is waiting. Three single-user computer stations, located just outside the Recording Studio, can also be reserved for digital projects. Software on these computers and the Recording Studio computer may include:
  • Tinkercad
  • Sketch-up Pro
  • Blender
  • Unity
  • Adobe Creative Cloud
  • Final Cut Pro
  • Compressor 4
  • Motion 5
  • iMovie
  • Garageband
  • iWorks
  • Manga Studio 5
  • Camtasia Studio (Mac)
  • Logic Pro X
  • MainStage 3
Due to demand, the Recording Studio and additional computer stations may be given to other patrons if you are more than 15 minutes late for your reserved time. The Recording Studio may be booked online in advance, on a walk-in basis or with the assistance of library staff. The additional computers outside the Recording Studio can be booked by library staff over the phone or in person and on a walk-in basis.

Need help?

Please schedule your session at least five business days in advance for one-on-one assistance. We are not able to accommodate last-minute assistance requests at this time.
