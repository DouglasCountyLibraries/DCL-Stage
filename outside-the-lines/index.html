Welcome to the 4th Annual Great Douglas County Brew Tour!

Thursday, August 31-Saturday, September 30

Visit these great Douglas County establishments this September! Enjoy a special free event brought to you by DCL, get your passport stamped at each location, then bring your completed passport to any DCL location and collect your commemorative Great Douglas County Brew Tour glass.* Click the caps and cup for event info. Event registration required only where noted.
Ride the light rail? Look for us at the Lincoln Station, September 12 and 15, 3-6PM. Grab some freebies and get some great ideas for matching a delicious brew with your favorite book!
Post a photo of yourself with a Great Douglas County Brew Tour coaster or coffee sleeve and you could win cool prizes! Be sure to use the hashtag #picsforyourpassport and tag DCL in the post.

Douglas County Libraries Goes Outside the Lines

The Great Douglas County Brew Tour is part of Outside the Lines, which celebrates libraries' spirit of creativity and innovation. September 10-16, libraries across the globe will reintroduce themselves to their communities and get people thinking and talking about libraries in a whole new way.
* Must be 21 or older. Visit at least five businesses to earn your glass. Glasses available while supplies last. Douglas County Libraries loves our patrons! Please drink responsibly. Get your glass at any Douglas County Libraries location (except Louviers).
