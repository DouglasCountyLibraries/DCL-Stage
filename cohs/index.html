Career Online High School (COHS)

COHS is an accredited online high school diploma and career certification program designed to help adults ages 19 and up realize their educational and career goals.

Get Started!
Interested students who pass an online self-assessment, prerequisite course and interview may be eligible for a grant to complete their high school education. With assistance from the Colorado State Library, we'll award up to 25 scholarships to qualified learners — you could be one of them!
COHS is fully online, so you can complete courses on your schedule and at your pace. Students have up to 18 months to complete the program, but some graduate in as few as six months by transferring previously earned high school credits.
COHS supports you with access to a live, qualified academic coach for guidance and encouragement, and to help you choose one of eight high-growth career fields. In addition to core academic subjects, you'll choose courses in: certified protection officer, child care and education, commercial driving, food and hospitality, general career prep, homeland security, office management, or retail customer service.
Remember, if COHS isn't for you, we offer other ways to keep learning, including helping you prepare for the GED or another high school equivalency exam.
