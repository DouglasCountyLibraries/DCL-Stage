Toiletries Drive

Support Your Neighbors

Help the Douglas/Elbert Task Force meet the needs of Douglas and Elbert County residents.

Drop off toiletry donations at any DCL location (except Louviers) through Sunday, September 10. Toiletries needed include:
  • Toothpaste
  • Toothbrushes
  • Shampoo
  • Conditioner
  • Soap
  • Body Wash
  • Deodorant
  • Feminine Hygiene Products
  • Diapers (size 3, 4, 5, 6)
  • Baby Wipes
  • Laundry Pods
  • Toilet Paper
  • Paper Towels
  • Facial Tissue
  • Dish Soap
Cash donations and gift cards are also gratefully accepted.
