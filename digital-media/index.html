Digital Media

Use your library card number and PIN to access e-books, audiobooks, digital magazines, movies, TV and music for the whole family.

Having trouble? Fill out our Digital Media Help Form and get answers.

Your library card number is the 14-digit number under the bar code on your card, beginning with 2302500. Enter it without spaces. Your PIN is typically the last four digits of your phone number.

E-Books

OverDrive

Access a collection of e-books and audiobooks to enjoy on your PC, smartphone or e-book reader.

OverDrive Kids

Safely search e-books for readers 12 and younger, including read-alongs for preschoolers. Filter by format, subject, device or reading level.

You might want:

  • Libby app (Android, iOS, Windows) or OverDrive app (Android, iOS, Desktop, Kindle Fire)
  • Adobe Digital Editions (Windows, Mac) or OverDrive desktop app (Windows) for Adobe EPUB formats and transferring to readers
  • Kindle Reader app (Android, iOS, Windows, Mac) or Kindle Reader device (Kindle formats)
  • Amazon account for Kindle format titles

Hoopla

Instantly stream (or download via mobile app) audiobooks and e-books, digital movies, TV, and music, including kids' materials. There's no waiting, and items are returned automatically.

You'll need:

  • One-time setup of a Hoopla account using your DCL credentials

You might want:

  • Hoopla mobile app (Android, iOS)

TumbleBooks

Safely search for e-books and audiobooks, animated picture books, read-along chapter books, and videos for the younger set.

You might want:

  • TumbleBooks mobile app (Android, iOS)
