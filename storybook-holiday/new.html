Silly Old Bear's

Blustery Day

A Storybook Holiday

Sunday, March 26, 8-11AM, Highlands Ranch

Tickets: $20 Adults, $15 Ages 3-13
Ages 0-2 free with paying adult
Celebrate spring with Winnie the Pooh and friends! Kids can enjoy a Pooh-themed puppet show, crafts, and activities, and hunt for Kanga, Roo, and Piglet in the Hundred Acre Wood. Costumes are encouraged! A delicious catered brunch will also be served.
