Please join the Douglas County Libraries (DCL) Foundation on Colorado Gives Day, Tuesday, December 6, to help support our community and the future of our libraries. The DCL Foundation funds programs and services that reach youth, adults and families in Douglas County and across Colorado, and helps ensure the long-term success of our libraries.
You don’t have to wait until Colorado Gives Day to give—you may make a donation today! When you do, your gift counts toward our December 6 goal and receives a partial match from the $1 Million+ Incentive Fund. That means your dollars go further in support of your libraries.
JOIN US in building a legacy of programs and services that inspire a love of reading, discovery and connection.
