On display at all DCL locations, NOV 19-JAN 7

Plan your visit! Exhibit is open during library hours, no ticket or registration required.

Enjoy a wonder-filled holiday experience for the whole family! Our Forest of Stories exhibit displays more than 120 decorated trees, all dressed up in stories—from favorite children’s tales and classic literature, to crime thrillers, mysteries and more. There is something for everyone, including an interactive scavenger hunt. Find certain ornaments among the trees and you could win prizes!

Visit all seven locations and discover the joy of stories.
