Summer Reading Program for All Ages

June 3-July 31

Hey kids! Starting June 3, print a reading log or sign up online, then read for 720 minutes or complete fun alternative activities to complete the Summer Reading Program. When you're done, visit us in person to get a free book (while supplies last)!
Summer Reading is for grown-ups, too! Readers ages 13 and up can read 900 minutes to finish and be entered into a gift card prize drawing. Print a bookmark or track your minutes and finish online.
Our friends in the community want you to keep reading this summer! After you sign up, visit any library location for free gifts from Chipotle, Colorado Rapids and Garden of the Gods.
