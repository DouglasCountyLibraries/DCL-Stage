Teens

DCL is here for you!

Looking for a place to study or work on a group project? Book a study room. Make the grade with special effects or a film you create in Parker. Use our research databases for papers and projects, to find information on scholarships, and to access live homework help.

Become a TLC Member!

Teens who want to make a difference in their library and their community are invited to apply to be members of the Teen Library Council (TLC). Earn volunteer credit, make a difference, have fun! Stop by a meeting or talk to a youth librarian to sign up:

Castle Pines

Meets the third Wednesday of each month from 5:30-7PM

Castle Rock

Meets the third Thursday of each month from 4-5:30PM

Highlands Ranch

Meets the first Thursday of each month from 4-5PM

Lone Tree

Meets the second Tuesday of each month from 5:30-7:00PM

Parker

Meets the last Tuesday of each month from 6-7:30PM

Roxborough

Meets the fourth Tuesday of each month from 5:30-7PM

Be a Reading Buddy

Applications for the next session open in 2024.
  • Buddies meet weekly for two months.
  • For each session, spend 30 minutes preparing, followed by one hour of reading and literacy games with your Little Buddy.
  • Volunteer spots are limited and your participation in the program is not guaranteed until your application has been received and approved.
  • Volunteer orientation and training are required.
  • Choose a location to complete an application.
