Foundation

Your giving makes a greater impact when you schedule it for Colorado Gives Day on Tuesday, December 5. All donations on that single day help us earn part of a $1 million incentive fund, ensuring that your gift goes further!

Schedule Your Donation Now
How do I do that?
The eighth annual Colorado Gives Day is presented by Community First Foundation and FirstBank. The $1 million incentive fund is one of the largest giving-day incentive funds in the U.S.

Make a Wish on 11:11

Starting November 11, look for the wishing wells at our libraries and make a wish with your loose change. The Douglas County Libraries leadership team will match all wishing well donations on Colorado Gives Day, and those donations will help make wishes come true in our community.
