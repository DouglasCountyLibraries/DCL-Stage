Library Perks

Congratulations! Being a DCL cardholder means you have access to all sorts of fun perks, everything from free digital media, to free online classes (in crafting, software, language and more), to activities you can enjoy in real life, like museum and park passes.
Explore your options and do something new!

Get unlimited access to over 2,500 audio-guided workouts and programs led by expert trainers in running, strength, yoga, and 10 other categories.

Get Started Now
Create an Aaptiv account using your library card and PIN. Limited subscriptions are available.

Watch more than 1,000 DIY classes from renowned artists—anytime, anywhere. No pressure, just possibilities!

Get Started Now
Create your account using your library card and PIN, and start crafting!

Check Out Colorado

A limited number of backpacks containing a pass good for admission to any Colorado State Park are available for checkout from our welcome desks.

Nontraditional Items

We offer more than just books and movies! Check out a sewing machine, microscope, or drawing tool and more for your next project. Items are available on a first-come, first-served basis; ask a staff member for more information.

Adventure Pass & DCL Local

Our partnerships with area cultural institutions let you reserve free passes up to 30 days in advance. Plus, enjoy DCL Local: Use your library card for discounts at participating businesses!

Online Learning

These resources make learning a new language or new technology skills simple and convenient. Learn from the privacy of your own home—or your local library or coffee shop.

Mango Languages

Online language learning from German to Spanish, Russian to Mandarin Chinese. No downloads are required, and an app is available.

Lynda.com

Learn software, creative, business and technology skills from thousands of video courses taught by industry experts.
