Online Learning

These resources make learning simple and convenient, whether it's a new language, technology skills, or resume and career prep. Learn from the privacy of your own home — or your local library or coffee shop.

Adult Learning Center

Live assistance from Brainfuse on passing the GED, preparing for the U.S. citizenship test, writing a resume, and getting help with Microsoft Office.

Brainfuse Homework Help

Brainfuse

Live homework help and tutoring, including a writing lab, skills building, test prep, and Spanish-speaking tutors, for students of all ages and learning levels.

BrainHQ

Fun brain games for relaxing and exercising your most important muscle for learning and memory.

Lynda.com

Learn software, creative, business and technology skills from thousands of video courses taught by industry experts.

News for You

Easy-to-read news stories from reputable news services to help improve English language skills. Includes audio, vocabulary lists, exercises and puzzles.

Mango Languages

Online language learning from German to Spanish, Russian to Mandarin Chinese. No downloads are required, and an app is available.

Check Out Colorado

A limited number of backpacks containing a pass good for admission to any Colorado State Park are available for checkout from our welcome desks.

Adventure Pass & DCL Local

Our partnerships with area cultural institutions let you reserve free passes up to 30 days in advance. Plus, enjoy DCL Local: Use your library card for discounts at participating businesses!
