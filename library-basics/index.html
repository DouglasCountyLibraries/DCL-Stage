Customer Code of Conduct
Get a Library Card
Register here to get a DCL library card, or to recover your library card number or PIN. See some of the perks that come with your new card! We also offer an organization library card—call us to learn more.
Request a Title
Can't find what you're looking for in the catalog? Here are a few more options.
Park & Pick-Up Holds
Use our curbside service to pick up holds at your preferred DCL location* following these instructions. Book Club Express kits may also be picked up using this service.
*Not available at Louviers.
Text Alerts
Text the word "SIGNUP" to 844-213-8348 to get account information via text. We’ll let you know when your holds are available for pickup, and when items are due or overdue. Standard texting rates apply. You may also manage other text alerts.
Homebound Delivery
Qualified cardholders can have library materials delivered right to their doors if they are unable to come to the library due to illness or disability.
Ageless Adults
Find an array of programs and opportunities for adults of all ages through our Ageless suite of services, including materials delivery for residents in participating communities.
Borrowing & Fees
Most materials check out for three weeks. Magazines and DVDs under 200 minutes check out for one week, and each customer may have up to 200 physical items checked out at any one time. Fees are assessed for lost items or special requests. Learn more about borrowing and fees.
Pay Fees
Technology Access
We offer free public Wi-Fi, public computers, and in-library laptop borrowing (library card required; unavailable in Louviers). Wide-screen Creation Stations with Adobe Creative Cloud are at all locations except Castle Pines and Louviers. A Recording Studio equipped with current, high-quality software and equipment is available in Parker.
Print & Copy
Print on the library's public printers during library hours regardless of device, network or location. Large documents and PDFs may take a few minutes to print.
Upload to Printer
Use the Princh website to adjust your print settings. Pick up at the library print release station.

Email to Printer
Choose to print the email contents, attachment, or both, in black and white or color, at the library print release station.

Prices
Print: Letter Size Only
  • Black & white: 10 cents per page; 20 cents per double-sided page
  • Color: 25 cents per page; no double-sided color printing
Copy: Letter & Legal Size
  • Black & white: 10 cents per page; 20 cents per double-sided page
  • Color: 25 cents per page; 50 cents per double-sided page
Copy: 11-inch by 17-inch
  • Black & white: 20 cents per page; 40 cents per double-sided page
  • Color: 50 cents per page; $1 per double-sided page
Please be advised that any print job not picked up at the print release station within two hours of being sent will be deleted. See a DCL staff member to pay with your Visa or MasterCard ($1 minimum).
Donate Books & More
Douglas County Libraries accepts donations of new and used books, music CDs and DVDs for ongoing and special book sales, or for further donation to organizations that distribute materials to underserved populations. Book sales benefit the Douglas County Libraries Foundation.
Book donations of up to two boxes are accepted during regular business hours at all locations, excluding Louviers. If your donation fills more than two boxes, please contact us to schedule a donation.
  • Please do not donate:
  • Items in poor condition
  • Textbooks older than five years
  • Encyclopedias
  • Magazines
  • VHS or music cassette tapes
  • Computer books older than five years
Dated receipts are available upon request; we are not able to quote a value for donated items.
Manage Text Alerts
  • STOP-to opt out of text alerts
  • ADDCARD-to receive notices for additional cards
  • RENEW-to renew materials
  • HOLDS-to stop or restart receiving Hold notices
  • OVERDUE-to stop or restart receiving notices about overdue items
  • MYBOOKS-to check the status of current items checked out
