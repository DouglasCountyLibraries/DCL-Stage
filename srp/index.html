Pull a Great Summer out of the Hat!

2022 Summer Reading for All Ages

Saturday, June 4-Sunday, July 31
Join us for a magical summer of reading, exciting activities, prizes, and giveaways. We’ve suggested 900 minutes of reading as a worthy goal, but if this challenge is not right for you or your family, feel free to create your own challenge! You can increase or decrease the number of minutes or set a goal for the number of books or pages you want to read. You can also do the supplemental activities we suggest on the reading log. We aren’t set on you reading 900 minutes, we just want you to read and have fun while doing it!

Help us meet our community goal of 8.5 million minutes read!

Watch the minutes materialize and help us meet our goal. If we meet our goal, the Douglas County Libraries Foundation will donate $1,000 to AllHealth Network, which works to enhance the quality of life for individuals and families in our community through compassionate, integrated behavioral health services.

Help your school earn $500!

All you have to do is sign up (and remind your friends and classmates to sign up!) and be sure to include your school name and your grade when you register. The school with the most sign-ups by July 31 will be awarded $500. Award recipients announced in August. See contest rules and restrictions, and schools currently in the lead.

10 Days of Giveaways

June 4-13

Sign up to win!
Look what we have up our sleeve! We’re giving away 105 prizes to Summer Reading participants who sign up to read. What could you win? Unlock the Magic of Summer Reading and find out!
Each giveaway day features a random drawing for that day’s prize. Everyone registered May 1-July 15 will be entered into the prize drawings. Most prizes mailed directly to winners or delivery will be otherwise arranged. Winners will be announced via DCL’s digital channels (first name, last initial).
