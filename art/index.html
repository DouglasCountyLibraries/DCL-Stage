Community Art on Display

Douglas County Libraries supports the arts by featuring stunning public art and donating display space at select branches for local artists.

Interested in Exhibiting Your Work?

Castle Rock

Ted Spring

Springtime in the Rockies features local photographer, Ted Spring, who has been delighting and serving the Castle Rock community for over 35 years. The exhibit is on display March 7 - April 8.
Visit Ted's Website »

Highlands Ranch

Ella Kinnersley

Ella is a 7th grader at Cresthill Middle School. She started sketching at age 8 and wants to become a professional graphic artist. She is influenced by My Little Pony artist Amy Mebberson and Denver illustrator/caricature artist Stan Yan, and inspired by graphic artists Vivienne Medrano and Alyssa Gerwig. Her exhibit is on display through March.
Visit Ella's YouTube channel »

Lone Tree

Shivani Raina

Shivani Raina is an upcoming artist in the process of exploring her creative side. Whether it is canvas, glass, or stone, her colors find expression in all. Her exhibit is on display in April and May.
