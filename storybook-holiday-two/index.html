A Storybook Holiday Halloween

The Legend of

Sleepy Hollow

"His appetite for the marvelous, and his powers of digesting it, were equally extraordinary, and both had been increased by his residence in this spell-bound region." — Washington Irving
Enjoy a fantastical evening in a favorite haunt as we celebrate the Legend of Sleepy Hollow and its spirited inhabitants. Your little ghosts and goblins will love our interactive, guided trick-or-treat experience, complete with treats and scares along the way, as well as spooky crafts, fun entertainment and activities, and lots of tasty goodies for all to enjoy. But beware the Headless Horseman!

Saturday, October 28, 6-9PM, Parker

$20 Adults, $15 Ages 3-13

(Ages 0-2 free with paying adult)
