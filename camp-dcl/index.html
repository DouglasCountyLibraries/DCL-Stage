Camp DCL

Spring Break Camps 2018

Be a Builder
Ages 6-12, $165 per session

Instructors from Craftsman & Apprentice will guide campers through building collapsible cardboard forts, wooden birdhouses, and experimenting with stone, brick and mortar. This weeklong, half-day camp includes daily ice breaker games and skills sessions related to daily projects.
  • Monday, March 19-Friday, March 23, 9AM-12:30PM, Castle Pines
  • Monday, March 19-Friday, March 23, 1:30-5PM, Castle Rock

Be a STEAM-y Chef
Ages 6-12, $155 per session

Cooking is both an art and a science in this weeklong, half-day camp. Kids will make two recipes each day and learn STEAM concepts like the science of taste and genetic modification.
  • Monday, March 19-Friday, March 23, 9AM-12:30PM, Lone Tree — Sold Out
  • Monday, March 19-Friday, March 23, 9AM-12:30PM, Parker
  • Monday, March 19-Friday, March 23, 1:30-5PM, Roxborough

Camp DCL

Summer Camps 2018

What will you be? Camp DCL's half-day summer camps provide unique learning experiences and hands-on fun for kids ages 6-12, giving them the chance to discover what they love. All camps include supplies and daily snack.

Be an Astronaut (Ages 6-12, $200, 20 camper limit)

Do you have the "Wright" stuff? This weeklong, half-day Space & Flight Academy camp with instructors from Mad Science is all about aerodynamics, the science of flight and more!
  • Moday, June 4-Friday, June 8, 1:30-5PM, Highlands Ranch, James H. LaRue
  • Monday, July 30-Friday, August 4, 1:30-5PM, Parker

Be a Chef: The Breakfast Club (Ages 6-12, $165, 20 camper limit)

Join The Breakfast Club and instructors from Sticky Fingers Cooking and make a new favorite breakfast recipe each day. Upside Down Pineapple Pancakes + Kid-made Brown Sugar Butter+ Pineapple Slushies=Oh, my!
  • Monday, June 4-Friday, June 8, 9AM-12:30PM, Roxborough
  • Monday, June 18-Friday, June 22, 9AM-12:30PM, Parker
  • Monday, June 25-Friday June 29, 9AM-12:30PM, Castle Pines

Be a Ninjaneer (Ages 7-12, $165, 20 camper limit)

Master the world of Ninjago: tame the Ice Dragon, motorize a Blade Cycle, design a Dark Fortress, and hone your Spinjitzu battle skills! In this advanced, weeklong, half-day LEGO camp with an instructor from Play-Well, campers will learn real-world concepts in physics, engineering and architecture.
  • Monday, June 11-Friday, June 15, 9-12:30PM, Highlands Ranch, James H. LaRue
#####################

Be a Robotics Engineer (Ages 9-12, $225)

This camp will encourage budding young engineers with fun, hands-on learning experiences, including the opportunity to build a robot! Get inspired by some of our favorite robots featured in these films.
  • Monday, July 17-Friday, July 21, 9AM-12:30PM, Castle Rock, Philip S. Miller — WAIT LIST
  • Monday, July 31-Friday, August 4, 1:30-5PM, Parker — WAIT LIST

Be an Artist (Ages 6-12, $150)

Does your child love the arts? Kids will try a new medium each day, bringing home painted, wearable and sculpted masterpieces for all to admire.
  • Monday, June 5-Friday, June 9, 1:30-5PM, Highlands Ranch, James H. LaRue — FULL
  • Monday, June 26-Friday, June 30, 1:30-5PM, Castle Rock, Philip S. Miller — FULL
  • Monday, July 24-Friday, July 28, 9AM-12:30PM, Castle Pines — FULL
  • Monday, July 31-Friday, August 4, 9AM-12:30PM, Lone Tree — FULL

Be a Coder (Ages 6-12, $225)

In our coding camp, kids will learn to create their own video games, among other coding skills. Check out these books to give your kids a jump on learning the ins and outs of coding.
  • Monday, June 12-Friday, June 16, 9AM-12:30PM, Highlands Ranch, James H. LaRue — FULL
  • Monday, June 26-Friday, June 30, 9AM-12:30PM, Castle Pines — FULL

Camp DCL will also be offered this summer! Watch this space or sign up for our eNewsletter for the most current information.
