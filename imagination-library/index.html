Dolly Parton's

Imagination Library

We're sending books to children in our community in partnership with Dolly Parton's Imagination Library.

Where's Dolly Wednesday?
We're partnering with local businesses to bring Dolly to the community!
Our life-size Dolly Parton cutout is on the road in Douglas County! Wondering where she'll be next? Here's where you can find her out and about in the community alongside Douglas County Libraries—stop by these businesses to hear stories and music, snap photos, and join the fun! Watch this page and your social media feed for upcoming stops.
Cottonwood Orthodontics, May 6, 10AM-12PM
Clothes Mentor, May 20, 10AM-12PM
Castle Rock Pediatrics, May 27, 10AM-12PM
Interested in being a future host partner? Learn more »
Through Dolly Parton's Imagination Library, children ages 0-5 receive and get to keep a free high-quality, age-appropriate book each month, sent straight to their home. We're excited to help create more opportunities to read together, build strong literacy foundations, encourage a love of reading, and increase the potential for school success.
It's easy and free! Register each child from birth to age five individually; every child in your Douglas County family may receive their own books. Let's inspire a love of reading together!
*Allow 6-8 weeks for delivery of your first book from Tennessee, after which books are delivered every month.
