The Douglas County Libraries Foundation presents its third annual

Friday, October 20, 2017 6-11PM
Highlands Ranch Mansion

Let us transport you through the rabbit hole to our version of Alice's Wonderland! Meet your favorite characters: perhaps the rabbit, the Cheshire Cat, the Queen of Hearts, or Alice herself. This will not be an ordinary gala—how can it be with such delightful adventures awaiting "through the looking glass?"
Wear your favorite theme-inspired outfit—or not—more casual attire is perfectly fine! Begin your enchanted evening with a stroll to the Mansion on the hill. Enjoy a delicious catered dinner, complete with a special dessert. Talk with authors, enjoy oh-so-different entertainment, and experience the fun of a silent auction, sure to satisfy your shopping fantasies. Leave with the wonderful memory of a favorite magical tale, knowing that because of your support, your libraries will continue to be great places for young and old.
