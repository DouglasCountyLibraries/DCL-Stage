September 28, 2018, 6-11PM
Highlands Ranch Mansion

Join the Douglas County Libraries Foundation as we travel to Neverland via the second star to the right, and straight on 'til morning! Meet your favorite characters: perhaps Tinker Bell, Wendy Darling, Captain Hook or Peter Pan himself. This is no ordinary gala! How can it be, with delightful adventures awaiting in the fanciful London and mysterious island locales of J. M. Barrie's imagination?
Wear your favorite theme-inspired outfit—or not—more casual attire is perfectly fine! Begin your enchanted evening with a stroll to the mansion on the hill. Enjoy a delicious catered dinner, complete with a special dessert. Talk with authors, enjoy oh-so-different entertainment, and experience the fun of a silent auction, sure to satisfy your shopping fantasies. Leave with the wonderful memory of a favorite magical tale, knowing that because of your support, your libraries will continue to be great places for young and old—and for those who never grow up.
