Business & Finance

Explore personal finance and investing, and delve into self-service market research and business development using the various resources here. You can also get free help using BizInfo, Douglas County Libraries’ on-site business development service.

    All Databases
    Students
    Business & Finance
    Lifestyle
    Online Learning
    History & Genealogy

Business Insights: Essentials

Business and industry overviews, rankings, market data, and investment and financial reports.

Business Source Premier

Economic data, company profiles, industry information and market research from more than 2,200 popular and academic business publications.

Colorado Grants Guide

Colorado Grants Guide Online

Profiles of local foundations and trusts, corporations, national funders, government agencies, and religious organizations. Access is available only in a library.

DemographicsNow

Demographic data useful for opening a business, finding audiences for products/services, and analyzing the needs of a given population.

Denver Business Journal

Online access to DBJ and its popular Book of Lists, which includes contact info for ranked Denver-area businesses. Access is available only in a library.

Directory Library

Gale Directory Library

Multiple directories for business research on companies, publishers, associations and more.

Funding Network

Foundation Center

Resources for finding funding opportunities, scholarships, fellowships and more, and researching philanthropic giving. Access is available only at the Parker location.

Global Issues in Context

Search by issue or topic to get specific global viewpoints, reference information, statistics, articles, reports and more.

Journals, Magazines & Newspapers

EBSCOhost

A collection of database resources with access to comprehensive information and thousands of full-text articles on educational, business, lifestyle and historical topics.

Legal Forms

Gale Legal Forms

State-specific, professional legal documents covering hundreds of legal subjects and issues, and an attorney directory.

Lynda.com

Learn software, creative, business and technology skills from thousands of video courses taught by industry experts.

Market Research

Plunkett Research

Worldwide market research, industry statistics, trends and in-depth analysis of top companies.

Media Market Rate & Data Service

SRDS

Market research tool cataloging thousands of media sources, including print and digital, national and local, and direct and broadcast.

Morningstar Investments

Data research and investment analysis on stocks, funds and industries.

Reference E-Books

Gale Virtual Reference Library

A searchable database by topic or subject, including the arts, literature, business, history and medicine.

ReferenceUSA

Business and residential info on over 25 million businesses and more than 262 million consumers in the U.S. and Canada.

Tools for Business

Resources to start and grow businesses.

Value Line Investing Research

Value Line

Investment and research tools for analyzing stocks, companies, industries, markets and economies.

Worldwide Library Catalog

WorldCat Discovery Services

Catalog of books and other materials from libraries throughout the U.S. and around the world.
