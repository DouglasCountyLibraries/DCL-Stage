Targeted search levels for children, young adults, and an adult reference center. Current events and primary sources are included.

Britannica in Spanish for high school and college-level research.

Business and industry overviews, rankings, market data, and investment and financial reports.

Colorado Grants Guide Online

Consumer Reports

Resource for consumer research on products and services.

CultureGrams

Illustrated resource featuring cultural and geographical information on provinces, states and world topics.

DemographicsNow

Demographic data useful for opening a business, finding audiences for products/services, and analyzing the needs of a given population.

Gale Directory Library

Multiple directories for business research on companies, publishers, associations and more.

Educator's Explora

Lesson plans, curriculum standards, and other professional development resources for educators.

Elementary School Explora

Full-text articles for elementary school-age children, including categories organized by popular topics.

Environment Complete

Full-text resource covering agriculture, energy, environmental law, natural resources, and more, with millions of records dating back over 100 years.

General Research

General Research Explora

A searchable database that uses various sources, including academic journals, magazines, reference books and more, to provide full-text articles on selected topics.

Global Issues in Context

Search by issue or topic to get specific global viewpoints, reference information, statistics, articles, reports and more.

High School Explora

Full-text articles from popular magazines and reference books, including categories organized by popular topics, for high schoolers.

History Resources: Facts on File

Research portal for American History, Modern World History, and Ancient & Medieval History Online.

EBSCOhost

Gale Legal Forms

Background information about writers from around the world, including essays on their work.

Lynda.com

Mango Languages

Online language learning from German to Spanish, Russian to Mandarin Chinese. No downloads are required, and an app is available.

Plunkett Research

Worldwide market research, industry statistics, trends and in-depth analysis of top companies.

Middle School Explora

Full-text articles from popular magazines and reference books, including categories organized by popular topics, for middle schoolers.

Modern World History

Resource for world history, from the mid-15th century to the present.

Morningstar Investments

Data research and investment analysis on stocks, funds and industries.

MyHeritage

Billions of historical documents, and millions of historical photos, for genealogical research, starting from the 16th century. Available from home.

Newspaper Source Plus

Digital resource of global news content, including millions of articles from newspapers, newswires and news magazines, plus television and radio transcripts.

NoveList Plus

NoveList K-8 Plus

Fiction and nonfiction resource for readers of picture books, chapter books, and young adult titles, including listen-alikes.

Opposing Viewpoints in Context

Background information, arguments and statistics related to current social issues.

Psychology & Behavioral Sciences

ReferenceUSA

Science Reference Center

Small Engine Repair Reference Center

Detailed, user-friendly repair guides and manuals for ATVs, marine and boat motors, motorcycles, snowmobiles and other small engines.

Social Sciences Full Text

Testing & Education Reference Center

Tools for Business

Value Line

World Almanac for Kids

Grade-appropriate content on various subjects and topics, as well as resources for homework help and teacher support.

Catalog of books and other materials from libraries throughout the U.S. and around the world.
