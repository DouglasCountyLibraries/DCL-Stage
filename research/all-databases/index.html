All Databases

Not sure where to start? Browse our A to Z repository of databases to find the research tool you need.

    All Databases
    Students
    Business & Finance
    Lifestyle
    Online Learning
    History & Genealogy

Academic Search Premier

Full-text article database of scholarly and peer-reviewed journals covering a variety of subjects, including biology, chemistry, psychology and physics.

Adult Learning Center

Live assistance from Brainfuse on passing the GED, preparing for the U.S. citizenship test, writing a resume, and getting help with Microsoft Office.

American History Online

More than 500 years of American political, military, social and cultural history.

AncestryPlus

Comprehensive genealogy and local history resource using census, vital and immigration records, and more. Access is available only in a library.

Ancient & Medieval History Online

The full scope of ancient world history, from prehistory through mid-15th century, including images and maps.

Auto Repair Reference Center

Maintenance info on thousands of vehicles from 1954 to the present, created by ASE-certified technicians.

Biography in Context

Biographical entries from encyclopedias and journal articles about noteworthy world figures.

Brainfuse Homework Help

Brainfuse

Live homework help and tutoring, including a writing lab, skills building, test prep, and Spanish-speaking tutors, for students of all ages and learning levels.

BrainHQ

Fun brain games for relaxing and exercising your most important muscle for learning and memory.

Britannica

Targeted search levels for children, young adults, and an adult reference center. Current events and primary sources are included.

Britannica Escolar

Britannica in Spanish for elementary and secondary school-level research.

Britannica Moderna

Britannica in Spanish for high school and college-level research.

Britannica Preschool Learning Zone

For early learners, a fun activity-centered environment that teaches letters, numbers, words, time, geography and more.

Business Insights: Essentials

Business and industry overviews, rankings, market data, and investment and financial reports.

Business Source Premier

Economic data, company profiles, industry information and market research from more than 2,200 popular and academic business publications.

Colorado Grants Guide

Colorado Grants Guide Online

Profiles of local foundations and trusts, corporations, national funders, government agencies, and religious organizations. Access is available only in a library.

Consumer Health Complete

Full-text resource of more than 250 health reference books and encyclopedias, covering mainstream and holistic medicine.

Consumer Reports

Resource for consumer research on products and services.

CultureGrams

Illustrated resource featuring cultural and geographical information on provinces, states and world topics.

DemographicsNow

Demographic data useful for opening a business, finding audiences for products/services, and analyzing the needs of a given population.

Denver Business Journal

Online access to DBJ and its popular Book of Lists, which includes contact info for ranked Denver-area businesses. Access is available only in a library.

Directory Library

Gale Directory Library

Multiple directories for business research on companies, publishers, associations and more.

Educator Resources

Educator's Explora

Lesson plans, curriculum standards, and other professional development resources for educators.

Elementary School Explora

Full-text articles for elementary school-age children, including categories organized by popular topics.

Funding Network

Foundation Center

Resources for finding funding opportunities, scholarships, fellowships and more, and researching philanthropic giving. Access is available only at the Parker location.

General Research

General Research Explora

A searchable database that uses various sources, including academic journals, magazines, reference books and more, to provide full-text articles on selected topics.

Global Issues in Context

Search by issue or topic to get specific global viewpoints, reference information, statistics, articles, reports and more.

High School Explora

Full-text articles from popular magazines and reference books, including categories organized by popular topics, for high schoolers.

History Resources: Facts on File

Research portal for American History Online, Modern World History Online, and Ancient and Medieval History Online.

Journals, Magazines & Newspapers

EBSCOhost

A collection of database resources with access to comprehensive information and thousands of full-text articles on educational, business, lifestyle and historical topics.

Legal Forms

Gale Legal Forms

State-specific, professional legal documents covering hundreds of legal subjects and issues, and an attorney directory.

Literature Resource Center

Background information about writers from around the world, including essays on their work.

Lynda.com

Learn software, creative, business and technology skills from thousands of video courses taught by industry experts.

Mango Languages

Online language learning from German to Spanish, Russian to Mandarin Chinese. No downloads are required, and an app is available.

Market Research

Plunkett Research

Worldwide market research, industry statistics, trends and in-depth analysis of top companies.

Middle School Explora

Full-text articles from popular magazines and reference books, including categories organized by popular topics, for middle schoolers.

Media Market Rate & Data Service

SRDS

Market research tool cataloging thousands of media sources, including print and digital, national and local, and direct and broadcast.

Modern World History Online

Resource for world history, from the mid-15th century to the present.

Morningstar Investments

Data research and investment analysis on stocks, funds and industries.

MyHeritage

Billions of historical documents, and millions of historical photos, for genealogical research, starting from the 16th century. Available from home.

Newspaper Source

Full-text resource of 45 leading national and international newspapers, as well as 315 regional newspapers.

NoveList

Comprehensive fiction resource with lists, recommendations and series information.

NoveList K-8

Fiction resource for readers of picture books, chapter books, and young adult titles.

Opposing Viewpoints in Context

Background information, arguments and statistics related to current social issues.

Reference E-Books

Gale Virtual Reference Library

A searchable database by topic or subject, including the arts, literature, business, history and medicine.

ReferenceUSA

Business and residential info on over 25 million businesses and more than 262 million consumers in the U.S. and Canada.

Science Reference Center

Full-text articles on biology, chemistry, earth and space sciences, and more from hundreds of science encyclopedias, reference books and periodicals.

Small Engine Repair Reference Center

Detailed, user-friendly repair guides and manuals for ATVs, marine and boat motors, motorcycles, snowmobiles and other small engines.

Testing & Education Reference Center

Online tests and prep tools for academic and professional exams. Also includes school scholarship info and a resume builder.

Tools for Business

Resources to start and grow businesses.

Universal Class

More than 500 online courses, from accounting to creative writing, photography to Excel.

Value Line Investing Research

Value Line

Investment and research tools for analyzing stocks, companies, industries, markets and economies.

World Almanac for Kids

Grade-appropriate content on various subjects and topics, as well as resources for homework help and teacher support.

Worldwide Library Catalog

WorldCat Discovery Services

Catalog of books and other materials from libraries throughout the U.S. and around the world.
