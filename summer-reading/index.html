Summer Reading Prizes!

Sign-up Prizes

Visit any DCL location to pick up your Summer Reading sign-up prizes, while supplies last. Summer Reading sign-up gift coupons and vouchers courtesy of Texas Roadhouse and Colorado Rapids.

Finishing Prizes

Collect a prize when you complete a challenge, or just for having an awesome summer! Kids can get a fun beach ball and free youth admission to The Patch in Elizabeth. Teens and adults can get a special-edition reusable book bag. Visit us in person to pick up your prize while supplies last!

Help us meet our community goal of 16,775 people signed up!

If we meet our goal, the Douglas County Libraries Foundation will donate $500 to Nature’s Educators, a top-rated nonprofit educational wildlife organization in Douglas County.
