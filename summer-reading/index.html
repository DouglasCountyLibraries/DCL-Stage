Hey kids! Starting June 2, print a reading log or sign up online, then read for 720 minutes or complete fun alternative activities to complete the Summer Reading Program. When you're done, visit us in person to get a free book (while supplies last)!
Summer Reading is for grown-ups, too! Readers ages 13 and up can read 900 minutes to finish and receive a limited-edition Douglas County Libraries book bag. Print a bookmark or track your minutes and finish online.

Attending a library event can make your summer in Neverland even more fun! Your Escape to Neverland Summer Reading Program registration does not register you for library events. Event registration opens 20 days prior to each event date. Events, dates and times are subject to change.

Escape to Neverland Kickoff Party

Face painting, activities, snacks, and much more!

Saturday, June 2, 10AM-Noon. All DCL locations. All ages. No registration required.

Escape to Neverland Events

Adventures in Art

Kids ages 6-12 will make folded, clock-eating crocodiles in this interactive art program, using guided drawing, oil pastels, markers and watercolor.

Adventures in Music

Kids ages 2-12 will sing, shake and shimmy their way around the world, enjoying songs, dances and instruments for hand-clapping, foot-stomping fun.

Animal Adventures

What animals live in Neverland? Kids ages 2-12 will join experts from Nature's Educators, plus live doves, toads, rabbits and owls, to explore where they live, what they eat, and how they survive.

LEGO Adventures

Choose from sessions for ages 4-6 or 7-12. Kids will journey to Neverland, dive into a massive collection of LEGOs, explore hideouts with mermaids and pirates, chase down crocodiles, and tinker with Tinker Bell!
