2018 Summer Reading Program for All Ages

Saturday, June 2-Tuesday, July 31

3 Reasons to Register Now for Summer Reading:

Skip the lines at the kickoff party on June 2 when you pick up your registration prizes and reading log.
Get early access to registration for Escape to Neverland library events (20 days prior to each event date). Summer Reading Program registration does not register you for library events.
Help your school win a $250 prize! Learn more »

Escape to Neverland Events

Adventures in Art

Kids ages 6-12 will make folded, clock-eating crocodiles in this interactive art program, using guided drawing, oil pastels, markers and watercolor.

Adventures in Music

Kids ages 2-12 will sing, shake and shimmy their way around the world, enjoying songs, dances and instruments for hand-clapping, foot-stomping fun.

Animal Adventures

What animals live in Neverland? Kids ages 2-12 will join experts from Nature's Educators, plus live doves, toads, rabbits and owls, to explore where they live, what they eat, and how they survive.

LEGO Adventures

Choose from sessions for ages 4-6 or 7-12. Kids will journey to Neverland, dive into a massive collection of LEGOs, explore hideouts with mermaids and pirates, chase down crocodiles, and tinker with Tinker Bell!

Escape to Neverland Kickoff Party

Face painting, activities, snacks, and much more!

Saturday, June 2, 10AM-Noon. All DCL locations. All ages.

Some rules and restrictions apply »
Contest applies only to K-12 students in schools affiliated with Douglas County School District. There will be one winning school at each level: elementary, middle and high. Sign-ups are tallied through Wandoo Reader online registration. The school at each level with the most sign-ups as a percentage of 2017-2018 enrollment wins. Contest runs May 1-July 31. Prizes courtesy of Douglas County Libraries Foundation. Winners will be notified in August 2018.
