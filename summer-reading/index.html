Castle Rock closed beginning May 22

Castle Rock residents may sign up online beginning May 1 or at an alternate Castle Rock Storytime location, and can attend a June 3 kickoff at any open DCL location to pick up registration prizes and logs, while supplies last. Prizes will also be available June 3-August 6, while supplies last, at any open library location or one of the alternate Castle Rock Storytime locations.
Castle Rock K-12 students are encouraged to participate in the school contest! Simply include the student’s grade and school at signup for the school to be counted.
Participants may attend Summer Reading events at any open DCL location.

Summer Reading Kickoff Prize Pickup

Visit the Summer Reading table at any DCL location, except Castle Rock, on June 3 from 10 a.m.-4 p.m. to receive a special, limited-time gift, while supplies last; available June 3 only when you show your Summer Reading email confirmation or Beanstack registration badge. You may also pick up sign-up prizes and reading logs, while supplies last. Registration for prize pickup is not required.

Contest Rules and Restrictions

Contest applies only to K-12 students in schools located in Douglas County and accredited by the Colorado Department of Education. Awards will be given to one school at each level: elementary, middle, high, and other (e.g., charter, K-12).
Sign-ups are tallied through Beanstack online registration. The school in each category with the most sign-ups as a percentage of 2022-2023 enrollment earns the award.
Contest runs May 1-July 31. Prizes courtesy of Douglas County Libraries Foundation. Award recipients will be notified in August 2023.
