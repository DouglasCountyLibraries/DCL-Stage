Minutes Read

Help us meet our community goal of 8.5 million minutes read!

Watch the minutes materialize and help us meet our goal. If we meet our goal, the Douglas County Libraries Foundation will donate $1,000 to AllHealth Network, which works to enhance the quality of life for individuals and families in our community through compassionate, integrated behavioral health services.

Help your school earn $500!

All you have to do is sign up (and remind your friends and classmates to sign up!) and be sure to include your school name and your grade when you register. The school with the most sign-ups by July 31 will be awarded $500. Award recipients announced in August. See contest rules and restrictions, and schools currently in the lead.

Contest Rules and Restrictions

Contest applies only to K-12 students in schools located in Douglas County and accredited by the Colorado Department of Education. Awards will be given to one school at each level: elementary, middle, high, and other (e.g., charter, K-12).
Sign-ups are tallied through Beanstack online registration. The school in each category with the most sign-ups as a percentage of 2021-2022 enrollment earns the award.
Contest runs May 1-July 31. Prizes courtesy of Douglas County Libraries Foundation. Award recipients will be notified in August 2022.
