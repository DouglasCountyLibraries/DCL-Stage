Help us meet our community goal!

If we meet our community goal of 8 million minutes read, the Douglas County Libraries Foundation will donate $1000 to the COVID-19 Recovery Fund through the Douglas County Community Foundation (DCCF).

Help your school earn $500!

All you have to do is sign up (and remind your friends and classmates to sign up!) and be sure to include your school name and your grade when you register. The school with the most sign-ups by July 31 will be awarded $500. Award recipients announced in August. See contest rules and restrictions, and schools currently in the lead.

Summer Reading Prizes

Sign-Up Prizes

Summer Reading sign-up gift coupons and vouchers courtesy of Pirates Cove and Parry's Pizza, while supplies last. Your Pirates Cove voucher can be used for the whole summer in 2021!

Finishing Prizes

Collect a prize when you complete a challenge, or just for having a great summer! Kids will receive a new book. Readers of all ages will be entered into a drawing to win one of 10 prizes: For adults, the prize is a stand-up paddleboard; for teens a 2021 season pass to Elitch Gardens; and for kids a 2021 season pass to Pirates Cove. Prize drawing winners will be announced August 3.

Finishing Prize Pick-Up

Kids prizes may be picked up July 6-12. Visit the curbside Summer Reading tent at our Castle Rock, Castle Pines, Highlands Ranch, Lone Tree, Parker or Roxborough locations to pick out a book, while supplies last.

Curbside Pick-Up Hours July 6-12:

Monday-Friday 10AM-2PM
Tuesday & Thursday Evenings 4-6PM
Saturday 12-4PM
Sunday 1-3PM

Contest Rules and Restrictions

Contest applies only to K-12 students in schools affiliated with Douglas County School District. Awards will be given to one school at each level: charter, elementary, middle and high.
Sign-ups are tallied through Wandoo Reader online registration. The school at each level with the most sign-ups as a percentage of 2019-2020 enrollment earns the award.
Contest runs May 1-July 31. Prizes courtesy of Douglas County Libraries Foundation. Award recipients will be notified in August 2020.
