The Brew Tour returns in August 2024!

Join us for our annual tour of Douglas County breweries and coffee shops, plus interactive events hosted at our libraries and select brewery locations.
Participants log visits to the different partner establishments and attend events to earn points toward awesome prizes, and to get entered into our Happy Brew Year Grand Prize drawing. Good things are brewing at DCL!
