BReW ToUR

August 1-September 30

Visit local breweries, earn prizes!

Our 2024 Brew Tour includes some great Douglas County breweries and coffee shops. Beginning August 1, log your visits to the different participating partner establishments to earn points, unlock rewards, and get entered into our Grand Prize drawing.
RegisterSign In
*Participants must register and log visits on Beanstack to be eligible for all prizes and rewards. Events and Power Hours each have unique keywords obtained on-site for points to be credited. Grand Prize drawing will be held in October.

Prizes & Rewards

Gather points and unlock rewards by logging your visits to each participating partner brewery and coffee shop. Each visit also earns you one entry into our Grand Prize drawing for a Happy Brew Year—one premium stainless steel growler with a booklet of fills from our participating partner breweries.
You may also earn additional points and unique rewards by attending Power Hours and DCL-hosted Brew Tour events at our libraries and select brewery locations.** Have fun with Bookish Trivia, Books & Authors Bingo, Scavenger Hunt, and more.
5 points each location visit
5 points each Power Hour visit
10 points each event attended
