Funding the Future

Giving Now for Generations to Come

Now more than ever, Douglas County Libraries is thankful to be part of a community that has generously supported and advocated for the Douglas County Libraries Foundation over the years. Let’s face it, 2020 hasn’t been easy. Yet, there is still much to be grateful for and much to look forward to. We hope you’ll look forward with us, as well, by helping fund the future on Colorado Gives Day, Tuesday, December 8.
Your donation supports the work of the Foundation in funding programs that reach youth, adults and families in Douglas County and across Colorado—today and for generations to come. And when you schedule your donation for December 8, your gift gets a boost thanks to the $1 Million Incentive Fund.
Schedule Now

We love to sing the praises of the enriching programs and services that make an impact in our community. They wouldn’t be possible without your generous support! We’re especially grateful for:

Our Community

Thank you for participating in the continuing tradition of Summer Reading, encouraging kids to begin reading while young and to keep reading during school breaks, so they grow up with a strong foundation in literacy.

New Services

We were thrilled to serve our customers by delivering at-home experiences and celebrations through DCL Delivers during our closure. And we’re delighted to continue that service today, even as DCL has reopened.

Opportunities to Build Bright Futures

Thank you for supporting our vision to inspire a love of reading and build bright futures through Cuddle Up & Read, 1000 Books Before Kindergarten, Storytime, Bark for Books, and Reading Buddies.

Community Partnerships

We’re thankful for the opportunity to inspire a love of discovery and connection in Douglas County through Exploration Stations with community partners.

Scholarship Support

Thanks to Foundation scholarships and financial support, kids in families facing financial hardship can play and learn at Camp DCL, and adults are able to achieve their high school graduation goals through high school equivalency and Career Online High School.
"Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much." – Helen Keller
It takes a community to make an impact for generations to come. We need you! Schedule your donation today.
