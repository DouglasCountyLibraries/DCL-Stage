Kid-friendly Fun:

The Market

The library’s farm-to-table play market and kitchen lets kids cook up all kinds of imaginative fun, from shopping for farm fresh fruits and veggies, to ringing them up in the market, to cooking up something playfully delicious in the kitchen. With kid-friendly shopping baskets, bright produce, weight scales, checkout stands, and a galley kitchen stocked with aprons, kids will have a blast playing shopper, grocery checker, and chef all in the same day!

Art on Display

Lisa Vetowich

May 1-June 30

Lisa Vetowich studied art at The REAL Art Academy of Colorado and trained under the tutelage of Living Master Ali Ghassan. Lisa's artistic goal is to bring the beauty of our world to life with great feeling. She lives just outside of Denver and enjoys the immense beauty of the Rocky Mountains.
Douglas County Libraries supports the arts by featuring stunning public art and donating display space at select branches for local artists.
