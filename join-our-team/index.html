Welcome to Douglas County Libraries, where exciting things happen every day at our seven library locations.

When you join our team, you become part of a dynamic network of doers. Differing talents and diverse backgrounds are valued. Yes, we work hard, but we also infuse fun (and integrity and excellence) into everything we do. And what we do is elevate our community by inspiring a love of reading, discovery and connection. You, too, can contribute to this narrative.

Our Story

Douglas County Libraries elevates our community by inspiring a love of reading, discovery and connection. It's a place where purpose and profession align and you're empowered to make a positive difference.
Our core values are the heart of our organization's culture. They guide the work we do, and also the way we interact with each other and our customers.
We're readers and leaders who work together in all manner of creative, impactful ways to meet goals and fulfill our vision. It's a great story to be part of!

A Collection of Perks

Work-Life Balance

We love our work, and we work hard. But we also have generous paid time off and floating holidays, so it’s possible to enjoy everything else in life too.

Compensation & Security

Earning now while saving for later makes for a happy ending when you close the book on your career. Douglas County Libraries offers competitive salaries, a pension plan through Colorado Public Employees’ Retirement Association (PERA), and an optional 401(k).

Health & Well-Being

Our comprehensive, competitive benefits package includes health, dental, vision, disability and life insurance; extended illness pay; and an employee assistance program. And we're a Health Links Certified Healthy Business—we really do care about the well-being of our employees!

Growth & Development

There’s room for professional growth and promotion through organizational development, training programs, and tuition assistance. Come grow with us!
