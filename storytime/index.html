Early literacy is what children know about reading and writing before they can actually read or write. Storytime nurtures six important pre-reading skills: narrative skills, phonological awareness, letter knowledge, print motivation, vocabulary, and print awareness.
Storytime at Douglas County Libraries emphasizes five simple activities — reading, talking, writing, singing and playing — that help children learn the skills to be ready to read. You can build on your child’s learning by incorporating these activities into your day as well — maybe you already are!
